Introl SLIM Series

Introl “SLIM” Series of Digital Motor Controllers are designed to control Velocity/Torque and Position of a Single Axis Brushless Servo Motor (optional DC Brush Type). These Controllers with programmable/ assignable optically isolated Inputs/Outputs and Analog Inputs/Outputs can eliminate a need for an additional programmable logic controller (PLC). A Windows-based PC Software (furnished with each unit), provides a simple “drag and drop” program building environment for a stand alone or multi-axis control scheme. There is no additional controller card needed to perform motion profiles for indexing, positioning and input-output processing. The unique software and firmware development scheme allows for all the drive functions, as well as the profiling to be performed by the same processor. This translates to a very accurate torque and speed control, since there is virtually no delay in processing. Use of the same processor to perform the motion controller task and the drive function have eliminated the extra hardware needed to support the motion control and I/O communication with the drive section. SLIM series can be powered from an AC or DC power source with power ranging from 5 to 75 Arms continuous and 18 to 500 Vac or 24 to 700 Vdc.

The SLIM Series Controllers/Drives are available with either Encoder and Hall (or commutating encoder) feedback or Resolver feedback for commutation and positioning. SLIM series can communicate with other devices such as PCs, HMIs or PLCs via their multi-drop RS485 port using Modbus RTU Protocol for programming, commands and monitoring machine functions and I/O’s.

To maximize the performance and functionality, the inputs are assignable/programmable to function as a fixed assignment, such as Jog Fwd/Jog Rev, Over-travel Limits, BCD or as general purpose inputs.

Applications

Press feed (cut to length with registration feed indexing option), electronic gearing (machine synchronization and ratioed follower), positioning tables (welding table, assembly lines), packaging machinery, screw feed to torque.

Specifications

  • Input Power:
    • SLIM-05-230: 70 to 250 Vac single phase, 50/60 Hz. or 100 to 360 Vde, 10 Amp max.
    • SLIM-08-230: 70 to 250 Vac single/3 phase 50/60 Hz. or 100 to 360 Vdc, 12 Amp max.
    • SLIM-15-230: 70 to 250 Vac single/3 phase 50/60 Hz. or 100 to 360 Vdc, 20 Amp max.
    • SLIM-25-230: 70 to 250 Vac single/3 phase or 100 to 360 Vde, 30 Amp max. Output derated to 17 Amp with single phase power input.
    • SLIM-35-230: 70-250 Vac single/3 phase or 100 to 360 Vde, 40 Amp max. Output derated to 17 Amp with single phase power input.
    • SLIM-08-460: 180 to 500 Vac single/3 phase or 200 to 700 Vdc, 12 Amp max.
    • SLIM-20-460: 180 to 500 Vac single phase or 3 phase or 200 to 700 Vdc 25 Amp max. Output derated to 8 Amp with single phase power input.
    • SLIM-50-460: 180 to 500 Vac 3 phase or 200 to 700 Vdc 70 Amp max.
    • SLIM-75-460: 180 to 500 Vac 3 phase or 200 to 700 Vde 100 Amp max.

      • 24 Vde versions are also available.

  • Power On Inrush: Internally limited by soft start circuitry to 5 A. max. peak.
    • Output Power
    • SLIM-05-230: 5 Arms cont., 10 Arms peak
    • SLIM-08-230: 8 Arms cont., 16 Arms peak
    • SLIM-15-230: 15 Arms cont., 30 Arms peak
    • SLIM-25-230: 25 Arms cont., 50 Arms peak
    • SLIM-35-230: 35 Arms cont., 70 Arms peak
    • SLIM-08-460: 8 Arms cont., 16 Arms peak
    • SLIM-20-460: 20 Arms cont., 40 Arms peak
    • SLIM-50-460: 50 Arms cont., 100 Arms peak
    • SLIM-75-460: 75 Arms cont., 150 Arms peak
  • Modulation Frequency: 17 kHz. To 6 kHz, based on model.
  • Serial Communication: RS485 (optional RS232) at 19200 baud with Modbus RTU protocol for setup and monitoring. (Up to 32 Controllers can be connected on a single BUS.)
  • Standard Protection:
    • On-board line fuses (SLIM-50, -75 have a circuit breaker)
    • Over-current
    • Motor short line to line or line to ground
    • Motor over-temperature
    • Hi Bus voltage
    • Low Bus voltage
    • Input power interrupt
  • Discrete Inputs: Optically isolated common cathode inputs. (10 to 30 Vde inputs at 10 mA minimum current.)
      Four fixed assignment inputs:
    • Controller Enable / Disable
    • Home limit switch
    • Sequence / RUN
    • Motor thermal switch

    Seven assignable/programmable inputs can be used as general purpose inputs, or assigned as +JOG, -JOG, +Travel limit, -Travel limit and BCD inputs or mixed combinations.

  • Discrete Outputs: Optically isolated common collector transistor outputs (5 to 30 Vde outputs at 20 mA max.), two fixed assignment outputs.
    • In-position/within programmed tolerance.
    • Controller Fault

    Seven general purpose programmable outputs.

  • Analog Inputs: Programmable/Assignable
    • +/- 10 Vde Input, 12 bit resolution differential
    • 0 to +5 Vde Input, 12 bit resolution single ended
  • Analog Output: 0 to +5Vde at 20mA max., 12 bit resolution proportional to motor current/torque command.
  • Hall/Encoder/(Commutating Encoder) Feedback Version: Differential line receiver with digital filtering (resolutions 200 to 10,000 PPR quadrature), On board +5Vde at 200 mA encoder power output. Contact us for specific requirements.
  • Resolver Feedback Version: On-board resolver excitation 4 kHz at 100 mA max. with differential sine/cosine Inputs. Simulated encoder 1024 PPR quadrature output.
  • OPERATING CONDITIONS: 0° to 55° Celsius ambient temperature non-condensing
  • Physical Dimensions:
    SLIM-05: 2.6°W x 9.0"H x 4.8°D
    SLIM-08, 15-230:   3.4°W x 10.2”H x 6.5"D
    SLIM-20, 25, 35: 4.5°W x 14.4"H x 8.6"D
    SLIM-S50, 75: 9.8°W x 24.0"H x 10.6"D
  • Program Storage: Drive/Controller set up parameters are stored in a non-volatile EEPROM with a capacity of 999 program segments.
